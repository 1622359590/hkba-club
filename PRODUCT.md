# Product

## Register

product

## Users
HKBA serves public visitors, blockchain industry members, partners, media, and association administrators. Visitors need to understand the association's credibility, people, news, events, and membership path quickly. Administrators need a calm CMS surface for maintaining banners, news, events, team profiles, members, messages, and settings.

## Product Purpose
HKBA Club is the official web and content-management platform for the Hong Kong Blockchain Association. Success means the public site feels credible and alive, while administrators can keep institutional content current without friction.

## Brand Personality
Institutional, future-facing, trustworthy. The experience should feel like a serious industry association with enough digital energy to belong in blockchain and Web3.

## Anti-references
Avoid lifeless grayscale partner walls, cramped profile cards, generic AI landing-page gloss, emoji-heavy admin dashboards, and any button that looks clickable but has no useful action.

## Design Principles
1. Make authority visible through people, partners, activity, and clear editorial hierarchy.
2. Keep operational surfaces dense, calm, and predictable for repeated admin work.
3. Use motion and contrast to support confidence, not decoration.
4. Every control should communicate where it goes or what it changes.
5. Preserve bilingual clarity across public and admin content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast where practical, keep keyboard focus states visible, avoid relying on color alone for state, and respect reduced-motion preferences for decorative transitions.
